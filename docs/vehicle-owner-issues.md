The vehicle intermitently reports a shifter error and if the vehicle stopped will put it in park and lock the shifter knob.

Troubleshooting that had no effect steps:
- removed aux battery
- removed auto stop start eliminator (physical device)
- computer reset: i.e. holding down the gas peddle for a certain time

What does clear the issue
- turn off car
- remove negative battery terminal connection
- remove small charge sensor connector from negative termina connector
- start car
- reconnect small charge sensor connector

Things checked
- Used o-scope on CAN bus, no extra noise observed
- Checked resistance form CAN pins to ground, 60 ohms, i.e. no ground faults
